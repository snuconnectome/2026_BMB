## ⏱️ Master Chronometry (Total Duration: 75 Minutes)

### Prologue: The Grand Disruption (0:00 - 0:10 | 10 mins)

- **0:00 - 0:02 | The Silent Entrance:** Display only a single quote on the screen: *"배움의 유일한 화폐는 예측 오차(Prediction Error)다."*
- **0:02 - 0:07 | Welcome to the Predictive Gym:** Formal introduction by Professor Cha. Use `class_01_0_syllabus.md` to explain the "Inside-Out" philosophy and grading system.
- **0:07 - 0:10 | The Oxford Bifurcation:** Briefly introduce the grading system—emphasizing that traditional exams are merely 40%, while the 60% Formative Agentic Portfolio (Audit Logs) is where true intellect is forged.

### Movement 1: The Neuroscience of Agency (0:10 - 0:35 | 25 mins)

*Incorporating "Inside-Out" and "Action Machine" materials.*

- **0:10 - 0:17 | The Grand Illusion (Inside-Out):** Discarding the camera metaphor. Introduce the brain as a *Preconfigured Dictionary* and a *Homeric Storyteller*. We hallucinate reality.
- **0:17 - 0:25 | Grounding Through Action (The Action Machine):** How the brain tests its hallucinations. Introduce *Corollary Discharge* (the cricket's silence) and *Active Inference*. Sensation without action is meaningless.
- **0:25 - 0:35 | The Giants' Shoulder Protocol (from `giants_zero_friction_guide.md`):**
  - Translate 'Action Machine' theory into course reality.
  - **The Action:** Creating a Persona (The Giant).
  - **The Prediction Error:** AI's limitations and hallucinations.
  - **The Update (Steering):** The Audit Log as the student's active correction of the world model.
  - *Crucial:* Explain that AI is not an oracle; it is an accelerator for the Action -> Prediction Error -> Feedback loop.

### Movement 2: Mapping the Cognitive Baseline - AI Literacy Survey (0:35 - 0:45 | 10 mins)

- **0:35 - 0:38 | Framing the Survey:** Explain that AI literacy is not about "which buttons to press," but about *cognitive sovereignty*. The survey measures their current reliance on passive learning vs. active steering.
- **0:38 - 0:45 | Live Survey Execution:** Students scan a QR code on the screen to complete the **[2026 BMB AI Literacy & Agency Survey](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd9yOGjonIMeAzDpgfsDiJZxAIu93qDZY4rAzZMKWwq-G7img/viewform)**. (The survey assesses their prior experience with AI, their comfort with uncertainty, and their epistemic reliance).

### Movement 3: Agentic AI Onboarding & The Final Boss (0:45 - 0:65 | 20 mins)

- **0:45 - 0:55 | The Onboarding Protocol (`class_01_1_agentic_orientation.md`):**
  - Instruct students to access the dedicated NotebookLM workspace.
  - Assign the first live "Action": Have Cline or NotebookLM summarize 5 recent neuroscience papers.
- **0:55 - 0:65 | The Final Boss: The Audit Log:**
  - Reiterate that grading focuses entirely on how viciously they cross-examined the AI's outputs (Steering), not the outputs themselves.
  - Review the Audit Log format one last time as the 'Academic Currency' of the term.

### Epilogue: Cultivating the Agent (0:65 - 0:75 | 10 mins)

- **0:65 - 0:72 | AlphaGo Homework Briefing (`class_01_2_homework.md`):**
  - The "First Observation" task: Watching AlphaGo.
  - Recording the Brain (Body), Mind (Emotion/Prediction), and Behavior (Action) in response to the '37th move' moment.
  - Explain the 3-step AI Sparring process (Data -> Analysis -> Essay).
- **0:72 - 0:75 | Final Q&A and Dismissal:** Open the floor for challenges, not just questions. Establish the intellectual rigor expected for the rest of the term.
